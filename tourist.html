Tourist Places

Explore Jakarta's culture and history! Here are Jakarta's best tourist places.

  • National Monument: Also known as Monas, this monument is one of the landmarks of Jakarta. It stands for the fight for Indonesia's independence.
  • Taman Mini Indonesia Indah: It is a culture-based recreational area located in East Jakarta.
  • Ragunan Zoo: Located in South Jakarta, it is a favorite attraction for families. It houses a variety of animals and species from around the world.
  • Ancol Dreamland: It is the largest integrated tourism area in South East Asia, including a theme park, hotels, beaches, SeaWorld, and more.
