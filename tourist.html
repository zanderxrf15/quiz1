Tourist Places

Explore Jakarta's culture and history! Here are Jakarta's best tourist places.

  • National Monument: Also known as Monas, this monument is one of the landmarks of Jakarta. It stands for the fight for Indonesia's independence.
  • Taman Mini Indonesia Indah: Culture-based recreational area located in East Jakarta.
  • Ragunan Zoo: Located in South Jakarta, it is a favorite attraction for Jakartans. It houses a variety of animals and species from around the world.
  • Kepulauan Seribu: also known as the Thousand Islands, is a group of islands located in the Bay of Jakarta. It is well known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life. The islands offer a range of activities such as snorkeling, diving, fishing, and island hopping.
