Local Food in Jakarta

Jakarta and food?! Explore Jakarta's cuisine here!

Jakarta, being the melting pot of Indonesia, has a vast variety of local cuisine. Here are a few:

  • Kerak Telor: A traditional spicy omelette dish in Indonesian cuisine. It is made from glutinous rice cooked with egg and served with shredded coconut and a dried shrimp topping.
  • Soto Betawi: The soup dish is filled with beef or beef offal, cooked in cow milk or coconut milk broth, plus ghee.
  • Nasi Goreng: Indonesian stir-fried rice that has a distinct flavor, usually added with kecap (sweet soy sauce) and accompanied by other ingredients, mainly egg, chicken, and prawns.
  • Gado-Gado: An Indonesian salad of slightly boiled, blanched or steamed vegetables and hard-boiled eggs, boiled potato, fried tofu and tempeh, served with a peanut sauce dressing.
