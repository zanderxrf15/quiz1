Local Food in Jakarta

Jakarta and food?! Explore Jakarta's cuisine here!

Jakarta has a vast variety of local cuisine. Here are a few:

  • Kerak Telor: A traditional spicy omelette dish in Indonesian cuisine. It is made from glutinous rice cooked with egg and served with shredded coconut and a dried shrimp topping.
  • Soto Betawi: The soup dish is filled with beef or beef offal, cooked in cow milk or coconut milk broth.
  • Nasi Uduk:Nasi Uduk is a traditional Indonesian dish made of steamed rice cooked in coconut milk and served with various side dishes. The rice is fragrant and rich in flavor, thanks to the addition of spices such as lemongrass, pandan leaves, and galangal. It is commonly served with fried chicken, fried tempeh, fried tofu, shredded omelette, and sambal (chili sauce).
  • Gado-Gado: An Indonesian salad of slightly boiled, blanched or steamed vegetables and hard-boiled eggs, boiled potato, fried tofu and tempeh, served with a peanut sauce dressing.
